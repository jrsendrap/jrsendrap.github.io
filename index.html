Juan Rafael Sendra

Mathematics · Symbolic Computation · Computer Algebra · Constructive Algebraic Geometry

I am a mathematician specializing in symbolic computation and computer algebra. My research focuses on the development of algorithms for constructive algebraic geometry, with particular emphasis on the manipulation of algebraic curves and surfaces and their parametrizations, as well as their applications to computer-aided geometric design, the study of algebraic differential equations, and symbolic matrix analysis.
